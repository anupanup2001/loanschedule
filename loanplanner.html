What is Loan Planner?

Loan planner is a flexible and intelligent personal finance tool (Loan Amortization Repayment Schedule calculator) which helps you be in control of your home loan, car loan or any other loan. It helps you easily visualize how your loan amount decreases gradually due to EMI (Equated Monthly Instalment) which you pay monthly.

Features

  • Shows how a pre-payment helps reduce your loan tenure
  • Impact of EMI change on loan tenure
  • Transparent view of Interest / Principal components deducted in each EMI
  • Supports interest rate fluctuation in case of floating interest loans
  • Provision to add additional loan (Useful for under construction building home loans)

How to use

  • Enter loan amount (or current principal remaining), Interest rate, EMI and start month of loan in input fields
  • Click on calculate
  • A Loan Amortization Schedule table is shown along with two graphs showing principal/interest breakup and Loan summary
  • Click on any row in the loan amortization table to add a one-time lump sum prepayment amount for that month
  • If you want to increase/decrease EMI from a particular month, click on the respective row in Amortization schedule table
  • Changes in interest rate mid way into a loan and also addition of loan amount is possible after clicking on Table row
