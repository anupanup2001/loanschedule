Save More

Pre-payment is a tool very few use. Well-timed pre-payments can end up saving you lakhs of rupees. Most employees are paid yearly bonuses. Use at least a part of this bonus to pre-pay your loan and save lakhs of rupees that you ‘donate’ to the bank as interest.
As an example, if you pay Rs. 50,000 after one year of taking a 25 lakh loan at 10.5% interest, you end up saving about 3 lakh rupees! Use the pre-payment feature in our tool to see for yourself.

Manage EMI

Few people realize the amount of money they pay as interest to the bank on their loans. By increasing the EMI that you pay on your loan by a small amount, you can reduce the tenure of your loan as well as save money.
For a 25 Lakh loan at an interest rate of 10.5%, you pay an approximate EMI of Rs. 25000 per month for 20 years. You end up paying around 59.6 lakh rupees. Now, assume the interest rate remains constant at 10.5%. By just increasing your EMI by 500, i.e. a total EMI of 25,500, the total amount paid reduces to about 57.1 lakh rupees. That is a saving of about 2.5 lakh. Not only that, your loan gets repaid faster by about 15 months!! Try for yourself by using our tool. Click the button below.
